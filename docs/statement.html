Statement of faith

The Holy Bible, consisting of Sixty Six books which is the fully and uniquely inspired Word of God, the only infallible, sufficient and authoritative rule of faith and practice.(2Timothy 3:15-17).

Only One True God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit. (1Timothy 3:16, 2 Corinthians 13:14).

The Deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His vicarious death and atonement through His shed blood, His bodily resurrection, His ascension, His mediatory intercession, His teachings and His personal return in power and glory. He is the only Saviour of mankind.(Colossians 1:15-23, 1Thessalonians5:23).

The salvation of lost and sinful men through regeneration by the Holy Spirit, salvation is by grace through faith. (Ephesians1:13).

The indwelling of the believer by the Holy Spirit, speaking in tongues, moving in manifestation & fruit of The Holy Spirit. By whom the Christian are enabled to live a godly life.(Acts 1:8, 2:4; 1Corinthians2:13).

The resurrection of both the saved and the lost; they that are saved unto the resurrection of life, and they that are lost unto the resurrection of damnation.(John 5:29)

The Spiritual unity of all believers in our Lord Jesus Christ, who comprise the Church, the Body of Christ.(1Corinthians 12:12,27)
