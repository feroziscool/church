Ministries

  • Youth Ministry

    By the grace of God, the youth have been active in/for the kingdom of God. They've been conducting children's summer camps, volunteering in church activities, being part of the church choir and also going to different places to preach the gospel and lift His name high.

  • Church Ministry

    For the glory of Lord Jesus, our church is involved in a movement to build up churches, in quantity as well as quality. We support the pastors spiritually, emotionally and, if needed, in monetary terms as well.

  • Women's Ministry

    Our women ministry is also a part of the network among Christian women in Delhi and outside, they meet, plan and participate to develop themselves in the kingdom of God.

  • Children Ministry

    We have taken a keen interest in the children's ministry. We have a weekly club for children aged between 4 to 14 years and also we have our Sunday school for the spiritual growth of youngsters. Many of the children, who were once part of Sunday school and club, are now an active member of church and also lead children younger than them.
