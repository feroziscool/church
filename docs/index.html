    • Living Hope Fellowship

    Living Hope Fellowship is a society, under which we have our church and other social activities. our ministry is about building up and strengthening the faith in one's life. its about guiding and giving a hope to the one who needs it. it started in 2001, by Ps Jordon Massey and other 20 people and by the grace of god we are still growing. today about 30 families gather every Sunday. we, maybe less in quantity but we are in good quality.

    Our pastor is involved in a pastor's network which is across India and also we have a branch, "jodoshram". by this our society is able to help in slum areas by giving education and "self-help" training, such as stitching, and making domestic items at home, etc. We also organize medical camps for the needy in slum areas.

    Well that was about our society, focusing on our church we have various ministries, youth, children, women, etc.
